Introduction to Computers

What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic machine that helps us do many things like writing, drawing, solving problems, and playing games.

Uses of Computers in Daily Life

  • Writing letters and documents
  • Watching videos and learning online
  • Playing games
  • Talking to friends and family
  • Solving math problems

Parts of a Computer

  • Monitor – Like a TV screen, it shows pictures and words.
  • CPU (Central Processing Unit) – The brain of the computer.
  • Keyboard – Used for typing letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Mouse – Used to click and move things on the screen.
  • Printer – Prints documents and pictures.
